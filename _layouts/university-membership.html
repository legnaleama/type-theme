What's included in Devex University Membership?

Through a small annual contribution on their behalf, your students and recent alumni will have free access to all of our most premium career services including:

The world's largest global development job board

Unlock 3,000+ opportunities each month including exclusive Devex members-only roles not found anywhere else.

Visibility with top recruiters

University Member profiles are highlighted in relevant recruiter search results, making employers more likely to see the students and alumni using your membership.

Expert-led career events

Connect your students to thought-leaders across the industry, and plug them into the latest career trends and direct-from-the-source insights on what recruiters are really looking for.

Industry leading professional development advice

From how to write a killer CV for the World Bank to how to ace an interview over Skype, Devex University Membership puts development-specific career advice at their fingertips.

Who should have a Devex University Membership?

We believe that every university offering undergraduate or graduate degrees in international relations, global development, public policy, sustainability, business, global health and related programs who are committed to helping their students find jobs before and after graduation should be part of our University Member network. These are just a few we’re already working with...

How do I get started?

Because we have designed University Membership to be flexible enough to partner with you at the program, school or university level, we like to talk to you first about your needs so we can customize a package just for you.
